Hero Coffee + Market

Located in Orchard Commons, Hero Coffee + Market is a bold & intrepid fuelling station that serves a variety of espresso beverages, pour-over coffees, teas, hot chocolate and smoothies.

Also on offer is a seasonal rotation of house-made and locally sourced pastries and desserts, along with a selection of gourmet snacks, grab-and-go meals and healthy treats

Did you know?

All coffee and tea served at Hero is Organic and Fair Trade. Our coffee beans are roasted on campus and we feature a rotating selection of single-origin beans from around the world.

Accepted here: Debit, Credit, Cheques and Internal Payment (Workday Cost Centre)
